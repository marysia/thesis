Master thesis AI guideline

	Fill in PEP form (thesis supervisor should be in agreement with course list)
	-> PEP form downloaded 
> Register Master Thesis AI in Datanose
	Link: https://datanose.nl/#project
	Info required: title, EC, Examiner, Assessor, Daily supervisor, description, start date, end date, date interim assessment.
> During the Master Thesis project
	Interim assessment, mid-term presentation and feedback session.
